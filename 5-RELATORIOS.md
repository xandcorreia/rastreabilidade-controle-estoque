# Essential Reports

## Batch Traceability
- Description of how batch traceability is conducted, including relevant processes and systems.

## Deposit Stock Levels
- Overview of current stock levels in each deposit, including metrics and analysis.

## Monthly Movements
- Summary of stock movements for the month, including inbound and outbound transactions.

## Loss Analysis
- Analysis of losses incurred, potential causes, and strategies for reduction.

## Compliance Reports
- Overview of compliance with regulatory requirements, including checks and audits performed.
